# Deleted Prefix Tricks

These prefix tricks were removed from the `tricks` table and moved to `trick_modifiers`.

| Slug          | Name          | Definition                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------- | ------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| anti          | Anti          | Anti added in front of side spin means the spin goes in the oposite direction as the wrap (clockwise spin for left leg front side, anticlockwise spin for left leg back side, and the oposites for the right leg) |
| backside      | Backside      | Opposite leg than a regular side                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| blind-        | Blind -       | There are two definitions of Blind... 1) Twist, Spins, or Grinds opposite the rider's comfortable direction... 2) Twists or Spins in the direction away from the front foot                                       |
| caught        | Caught        | feet land on pedals/cranks/tire and trick is completed while unicycle is still mid-air                                                                                                                            |
| fakie         | Fakie         | indicates trick begins with unicycle traveling backward                                                                                                                                                           |
| late          | Late          | trick begins after the rider and/or unicycle have left the ground, preferably at the peak or after the peak of the jump                                                                                           |
| out           | Out           | spin trick that starts from seat in between the rider's legs (seat in)                                                                                                                                            |
| rolling       | Rolling       | indicates trick is done while riding forward (as opposed to static)                                                                                                                                               |
| seat-in       | Seat In       | the seat is in between the rider's legs                                                                                                                                                                           |
| seat-in-back  | Seat In Back  | seat in behind; seat not between the riders' legs                                                                                                                                                                 |
| seat-in-front | Seat In Front | trick that initiates with the seat in front of the rider, not between the rider's legs                                                                                                                            |
| seat-out      | Seat Out      | seat is in front; seat is NOT in between the rider's legs                                                                                                                                                         |
| side          | Side          | both legs of rider are on one side of the unicycle during or at some point duirng the trick                                                                                                                       |
| static        | Static        | indicates trick begins from a static position (i.e. the unicycle is not rolling forwards or backwards)                                                                                                            |
| switch        | Switch        | With the less comfortable foot forward or hand on the seat (used by itself usually refers to foot position)                                                                                                       |
| switch-foot   | Switch Foot   | When the rider's less comfortable foot is in front                                                                                                                                                                |
| switch-hand   | Switch hand   | When the rider's less comfortable hand is used                                                                                                                                                                    |
| toeslide      | Toeslide      | Sliding the tip of your foot/feet over the ground or preferebly floor in stuff like onefootrides, forward scuffs, nopushrolls, seat pirouettes, etc                                                               |
