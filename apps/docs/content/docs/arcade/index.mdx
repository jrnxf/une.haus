## overview

the arcade is a side-scrolling runner game where you ride a unicycle through procedurally generated terrain, dodging obstacles and collecting letters.

## how to play

tap (mobile) or press **space** / **up arrow** (desktop) to jump. tap again mid-air for a double jump with a seat-spin animation. survive as long as you can while the speed gradually increases.

## obstacles

the terrain throws a mix of challenges at you: stairs, ramps, gaps, rails, spikes, bumps, trampolines, and the occasional scooter kid. hitting any obstacle ends the run.

## scoring

your score climbs as you survive. collect letters that spell "unehaus" for bonus points — completing the full word earns 2500 points. your all-time high score is saved to your profile.

## difficulty

the game starts at a comfortable pace and gradually speeds up the longer you survive, pushing from a casual ride to a frantic sprint.
