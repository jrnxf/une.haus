## overview

the feedback page lets you send a message straight to the developers. bug reports, feature ideas, or just things you'd like to see — it all goes here.

## how to submit

navigate to the feedback page (available from your profile menu). write your message and optionally attach an image or video to illustrate what you mean. hit submit and your feedback is sent via email to the team.

## media attachments

you can attach one piece of media per submission:

- **image** — inline upload with a preview before sending
- **video** — file upload with progress tracking
