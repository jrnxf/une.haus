## creating a post

posts have their own dedicated create page. add a title, rich text content, and optionally attach images, video uploads, or YouTube embeds. tag your post and [@mention](/docs/community/mentions) other riders.

## tags

tag your posts so others can find them: flatland, street, trials, freestyle, mountain, distance, random, memes, buy, sell, nbds, til, bails.

## browsing and filtering

filter posts by search query and tags. infinite scroll keeps things smooth as you browse.

## engagement

every post supports likes and threaded comments.

## editing

you can edit your own posts from the post detail page.
