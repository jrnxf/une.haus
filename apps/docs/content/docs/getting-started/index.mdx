## signing in

une.haus uses email-based login with no passwords. enter your email, receive a 4-digit code, and enter the code. that's it.

## new accounts

if no account exists for the email you entered, you're taken to a registration page to set up your rider profile -- name, avatar, disciplines, and more.

## the intro page

the [intro page](/intro) is a walkthrough of every major feature on the platform. it's worth a read whether you're brand new or coming back from skrrrt.io.
