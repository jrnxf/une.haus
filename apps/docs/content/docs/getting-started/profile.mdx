---
title: your profile
description: set up and customize your rider profile
---

## name and bio

your name is required. you can also add an optional bio that supports [@mentions](/docs/community/mentions) of other riders.

## avatar

upload a profile photo so other riders can recognize you around the site.

## disciplines

pick all the disciplines you ride: street, flatland, trials, freestyle, mountain, distance, or other.

## location

set your city or region to show up on the [rider map](/docs/community).

## social links

link your profiles from Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, and Spotify.

## editing your profile

you can edit your profile from the sidebar user menu or directly from your profile page.
