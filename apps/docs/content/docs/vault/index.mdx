## what is it

the vault is the entire unicycle.tv video archive, preserved and browsable. this is content from the golden age of unicycling, before YouTube and Facebook took over.

## browsing

scroll through the archive with infinite scroll and pagination. filter by riders, disciplines, and title to find what you're looking for.

## video detail pages

each video has its own page with metadata, rider attribution, comments, and likes.

## claps

quick engagement on videos -- give a clap to show appreciation.

## edit history

you can view the edit history of any vault entry to see how its metadata has changed over time.

## suggesting changes

if you spot incorrect metadata or missing rider credits, you can [suggest edits](/docs/vault/suggesting-edits).
