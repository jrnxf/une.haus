## how it works

follow a rider from their profile page. hit the follow button and you're done.

## what following does

- you get a [notification](/docs/community/notifications) when a rider you follow posts new content
- the rider you follow gets a notification that you followed them
- follower and following counts show on profile pages — click either count to see the full list

## unfollowing

unfollow from the same button on their profile page. the rider is not notified when you unfollow.
