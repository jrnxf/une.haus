## in-app notifications

you get notified when someone likes your content, comments on your posts, follows you, mentions you, or when someone you follow posts something new. notifications are grouped and browsable from your notifications page.

## notification types

each type can be toggled individually:

- likes
- comments
- new followers
- new content from followed users
- mentions

## email digests

opt-in email summaries of your notifications. choose weekly or monthly frequency, pick the day (day of week for weekly, day of month for monthly), and set the time in UTC. off by default.

## game reminders

subscribe to rack it up round start reminders. choose how far in advance you want to be notified: 12, 24, 48, or 72 hours before the round begins.

## unsubscribe

a single toggle unsubscribes you from all emails at once (except account-related ones). you can also unsubscribe via one-click links in email footers.

## settings

all notification preferences are accessible from the notifications settings page.
